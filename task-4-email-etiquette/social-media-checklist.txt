Responsible Social Media Use (College Student) -Checklist
Do’s
* Think before posting: Assume anything shared could become public or permanent.
* Protect your privacy: Use strong passwords, enable 2-factor authentication, and review privacy settings.
* Be respectful and kind: Communicate professionally and avoid harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
* Verify information before sharing: Check credible sources to avoid spreading misinformation.
* Maintain a positive digital footprint: Share content that reflects well on your values, academics, and career goals.
Don’ts
* Don’t overshare personal details: Avoid posting your address, phone number, ID cards, or real-time location.
* Don’t post when angry or emotional: Pause before responding to avoid conflict or regret later.
* Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling: It can escalate quickly and harm your reputation.
* Don’t plagiarize or repost without credit: Always give proper attribution for others’ work.
* Don’t let social media disrupt academics or sleep: Set time limits and avoid doomscrolling during study hours.
