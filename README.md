## 📧 Task 4: Professional Communication & Etiquette
I have documented and applied the following **Email Etiquette** rules to ensure professional communication within the academic and corporate sectors:
1. **Clear Subject Lines:** For immediate context and high-volume management.
2. **Formal Salutations:** Respecting professional hierarchies.
3. **The "CC/BCC" Rule:** Managing stakeholder transparency.
4. **Action-Oriented Closings:** Ensuring every correspondence has a clear next step.

---

## 🛡️ Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Online Safety
I researched a **realistic cybercrime scenario** (Phishing in Academic Institutions) to help peers recognize and avoid online threats:
* **The Threat:** Deceptive emails mimicking University portals to steal student credentials.
* **The Solution:** Verifying URL certificates, enabling 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication), and reporting suspicious links.
* **Key Takeaway:** Digital safety starts with a "Zero Trust" approach to unsolicited links.
